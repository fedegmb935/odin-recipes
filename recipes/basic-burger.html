A Simple Basic Beef Burger

Description

Simple and easy to male, this burger patties are made with ground beef and a mixture of bread crumbs.

Follow this recipe and add your favorite condiments to enjoy this summer meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill and oil the grate.
  2. Put egg, salt and pepper together in a medium bowl and start mixing. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and keep mixing with a fork until well blended.
  3. Use the mixture to form four patties.
  4. Place the patties on the preheated grill and cook them to the desired doneness.
